Let's start with an example. Here's how to multiply some HUGE numbers. Let's try 1,643 × 2,293!

According to the photo, the answer is 3,747,399. What a big number! Here's the check:

1,643 × 2,293 ≈ 2,000 × 2,000
              = (2 × 1,000)(2 × 1,000)
              = 2 × 1,0002
              = (22 × 1,0002)
              = 4((103)2)
              = 4(106)
              = 4(1,000,000)
              = 4,000,000
