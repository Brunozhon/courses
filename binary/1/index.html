--- layout: main title: Binary ---

In binary, we only have 2 values:

0
1

In our base (base-10), the places are these:

Millions (10 to the power of 6)
Hundered Thousands (10 to the power of 5)
Ten Thousands (10 to the power of 4)
Thousands (10 to the power of 3)
Hundreds (10 to the power of 2)
Tens (10 to the power of 1)
Ones (10 to the power of 0)

But in binary (base-2), the places are these:

Sixty-fours (2 to the power of 6)
Thirty-twos (2 to the power of 5)
Sixteenths (2 to the power of 4)
Eights (2 to the power of 3)
Fours (2 to the power of 2)
Twos (2 to the power of 1)
Ones (2 to the power of 0)
